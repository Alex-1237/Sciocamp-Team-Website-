Who is Srivant?

Srivant is a rising junior in High School North. Srivant Pothuraju was the captain for the Community Middle School Science Olympiad team for 2019-2020. He has also placed nationally in Science Olympiad, getting a 3rd overall score during 2018-2019 year. Srivant has a lot of mentorship experience from training current members of the CMS Science Olympiad team and teaching a bunch of Sciovirtual courses. He was on the WW-P High School North Science Olympiad team in 2020-2021, participating in primarily physics events (Machines, Circuit Lab, Sounds of Music). Srivant has helped Team Nayeon in several ways such as organizing practices and assisting members in need.
